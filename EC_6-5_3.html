기초클래스(Pattern-Focused Basis)
* TIP !
  • 1. 문법 틀릴 걱정 NO! 막 뱉기.
  • 2. 꼬리질문으로 ‘WHY?’ 많이 하기.
  • 3. 꼬리질문 할 때 상대방 답변의 ‘단어’
    하나를 잡고 그 단어에 관련된 질문을
    하기.
  • ex) A : 용준 always helps me.
  • B : Are you close with 용준?
  • A : I am close with 용준!
  • B : Why ?
  • A : We’re high school friends !
  • B : What high school did you go ?
  • ▶ Practice with your partner !
  • 답변영작 - 1. What do you hate about me ?
  •     ex) I hate that you 동사.
  • 답변영작 - 2. What are you excited about ?
  •     ex) I’m excited about 명사(혹은 동사ing) because 주어+동사.
  • 답변영작 - 3. What do you want ? Money ? House ?
  •     ex) That’s not something that I want ! I want 명사 because 주어+동사.
  • 답변영작 – 4. How long have you done that ?
  •     ex) It’s been 시간 since 과거문장.
